**Pushdown Automata**

**Exercise 1:**

Implement the following methods in the `Stack` class:

- `pop()`: pop.
- `push(Symbol x)`: push the symbol x.
- `replace(Word w)`: replace the top of the stack with the word w.
- `toString()`: represent the stack as a String.
